Welcome to Chez Fur

Chez Fur is a café that offers not just coffee and great food but also a chance to interact with a wonderful clowder of cats. The cats are all healthy and screened for general friendliness toward the fur-free two-legged guests of the café.

For guests lucky enough to be chosen by one of the cats (they do tend to be picky), adoption screening and placement is possible.
